# Roadmap

## Current: Setting up the repository

We're building out the foundation. This includes all core project files, documentation, the protocol specification, and the reference Tauri desktop application. Everything needed to understand, build, and contribute to the project will live here.

## Future: Asynchronous exchange API

The first major feature beyond the core protocol is an API that enables asynchronous exchanges between people and organizations.

The model: imagine a church, a mutual aid group, a community garden, a school, or any organization that relies on contributions from real people. A contributor submits a record of their work — what they did, how long it took — and the organization reviews and recognizes that contribution through a signed HEP record. Both parties hold a copy. No one needs to be online at the same time.

This pattern applies anywhere people give their time and energy to something bigger than themselves and deserve recognition for it.
